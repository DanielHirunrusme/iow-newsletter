by Goldwin 0




297×215mm (multiple formats), 1000 copies

Enquiry #1, #2, #3 presents three catalogues in the form of a book project, published alongside our wider collaboration with Goldwin 0 - an experimental fashion platform based in Japan. It documents a series of activations within various spatial, sonic and material contexts that investigate the liminal space where design, technology and art co-exist in harmony with nature.

The Journey of Things (Third Edition)
Magdalene Odundo




180×240mm×15mm, 2000 copies
ISBN 978-1-9160024-1-8
£40, purchase

The Journey of Things by Magdalene Odundo is made available once again for emerging audiences. In its third printing, the book reveals the unique characteristics that have granted it continued resonance.

The book features 44 of Odundo’s vessels, a large selection of museological and contemporary objects that reveal the range of global references that have informed her practice, alongside various texts including a conversation with Ben Okri.
